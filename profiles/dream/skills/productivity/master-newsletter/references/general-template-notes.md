# General Template Notes

## Hermes / Product Update

Recommended template:
- templates/hermes-update.md

Best for:
- release notes turned into readable email copy
- product update newsletters
- changelog summaries
- feature announcements
- internal platform update memos

Tone guidance:
- clear
- useful
- specific
- lightly confident
- not hype-heavy

Best structure:
- what changed
- why it matters
- how to use it
- improvements/fixes
- CTA to try it or give feedback
