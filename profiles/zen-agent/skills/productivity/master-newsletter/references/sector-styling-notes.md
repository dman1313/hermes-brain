# Sector Styling Notes

Use these notes when the user wants sector-specific newsletter/email styling.

## Education

Tone:
- clear
- trustworthy
- welcoming
- community-oriented
- informative without feeling institutional

Visual direction:
- clean academic/professional look
- blue-forward palette
- simple dividers
- strong readability
- straightforward CTA buttons

Content emphasis:
- program updates
- student/community outcomes
- deadlines and announcements
- events
- resources

Avoid:
- overly luxurious styling
- hype-heavy marketing language
- cluttered layouts

Recommended template:
- templates/html-email-education-template.html

### International school sub-style

Use this variant when the audience includes current parents, relocating families, or future students evaluating the school.

Additional tone notes:
- globally welcoming
- reassuring
- culturally inclusive
- confident but not elite/exclusive
- practical and family-friendly

Additional content emphasis:
- student experience
- parent communication clarity
- international community and belonging
- academic quality and pathways
- campus life, activities, and support systems
- admissions milestones for prospective families

Audience split guidance:
- Parent updates: prioritize clarity, logistics, dates, student wellbeing, upcoming activities, and concise action items.
- Future-student/family marketing: prioritize belonging, outcomes, campus experience, international mindset, and a clear next step.

Avoid:
- writing parent updates like admissions brochures
- writing marketing emails like internal admin memos
- sounding overly elite, cold, or corporate

Recommended templates for international schools:
- templates/international-school-parent-update.md
- templates/international-school-admissions-marketing.md
- templates/international-school-event-invitation.md
- templates/international-school-student-story.md
- templates/international-school-principal-letter.md

## Nonprofit

Tone:
- mission-led
- human
- sincere
- hopeful
- impact-oriented

Visual direction:
- grounded and warm
- green/earth-forward palette
- clean storytelling layout
- accessible typography
- clear donation/learn-more/volunteer CTA

Content emphasis:
- impact stories
- beneficiary/community outcomes
- donor or volunteer updates
- campaign milestones
- calls to support action

Avoid:
- corporate jargon
- over-designed luxury styling
- manipulative urgency unless truly warranted

Recommended template:
- templates/html-email-nonprofit-template.html
